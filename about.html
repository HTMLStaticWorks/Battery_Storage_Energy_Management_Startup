Our Foundation Story

Established in 2021 by a team of grid engineers and battery scientists, we recognized that localized storage was the key to unlocking the full potential of renewable solar grids.

What started as a material science research laboratory has grown into a global provider of smart energy modules, running thousands of active home and factory storage units.

Decarbonizing Grid Power

Our mission is to enable full localized grid resilience, matching green energy consumption profiles directly with solar production curves.

We envision a decentralized utility future where every commercial building and home operates as a self-sustaining grid asset, saving costs while offsetting greenhouse emissions.

Circular Economy Logistics

We hold ourselves to the highest ecological manufacturing benchmarks. Our battery cells are assembled using clean energy assets and sourced responsibly.

We integrate active battery cell end-of-life recycling procedures, ensuring 95% of metallic substrates (Lithium, Cobalt, Copper) are recovered and routed back to materials manufacturing loops.

Certified Cell Design Laboratories

Advanced materials chemistry defines our performance edge. We operate ISO-9001 certified labs to conduct load testing and cycle diagnostics.

Every battery module incorporates our proprietary Smart BMS (Battery Management System) which monitors battery health, regulates charging temperature thresholds, and optimizes energy storage life.
